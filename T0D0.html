New Jersey Limits Cellphone Use in Schools

SR
Sarah Reyes
Education Editor
Updated March 12, 2026 · 9:24 a.m.

New Jersey has become the latest state to limit students' cellphone use during the school day.

Governor Phil Murphy recently signed legislation requiring schools to restrict the non-academic use of smartphones and other internet-connected devices beginning in the 2026–2027 school year. Murphy said the policy is intended to reduce distractions and help students stay focused in class.
